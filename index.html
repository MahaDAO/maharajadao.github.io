What is a DAO?

A DAO stands for a Decentralized Autonomus Organization. It's a for-profit organization that is fully decentralized without any central authority in control. All the decisions made are taken democratically amongst the share-holders of the DAO.

What will the MaharajaDAO do?

The MaharajaDao will specifically support and promote DAOs/DApps that are 100% made in India.

By doing so, the MaharajaDao will allow for investors to get a share in products created by blockchain entrepreneurs/developers in India, at a massive scale.

Why launch a DAO for India?

Because in India the legality of cryptocurrencies is still not clear, which has made it very hard for entrepreneurs/developers to create blockchain-based products. If nothing is done, then this country will face a huge brain-drain in terms of blockchain talent.

By creating a DAO, these entrepreneurs/developers know that they will always get support from the DAO, irresepective of the decision made by the RBI on cryptocurrencies.

Can the MaharajaDAO ever be taken down?

No. All the power within the DAO is decentralized which means that no entity or individual (not even the creators) can take full control of the DAO.

This means that the MaharajaDAO is resistant to any attacks or corruption.

How will the MaharajaDAO work?

1. Investors invest in the DAO

By the means of an ICO, Investors put in money (Ether) into a DAO smart-contract that'll lock up the funds that'll then be invested into promising DAOs/DApps.

2. Developers/Entrepreneurs submit their projects for funding

All submissions will happen with the DAO's smart contract.

Projects that receive funding from the DAO need to emphasise that they'll their issue tokens to the MAHA token holders.

3. Token holders vote on the most promising projects

Once project submissions have been sent to the DAO, it'll then be analyzed by the MAHA token holders who'll then then analyze and vote whether or not to fund a project.

Voting is done collectively in a decentralized fashion.

4. Projects receive the funds

If a project has been approved by the MAHA token holders, it then receives funds from the DAO through the means of a DAICO.

This ensures that the funds can always be refunded back to the DAO in case the project performs poorly.

At the same time the project issues it's token to all MAHA token holders, proportionate to how much MAHA tokens they hold.

5. MAHA Token holders profit from the most successful projects

If a project becomes succesful, then the value of tokens given away to the MAHA token holders during the time of funding, rises in value.

What is the MAHA token?

The MAHA token is an ERC20 token on the Ethereum blockchain that allows tokens holders to vote on various decisions made within the DAO.

Where can I get MAHA tokens?

The MAHA token has not yet launched, but it will be distributed in many ways to ensure that token holders are as decentralized as possible.

You can easily buy MAHA token from an exchange (Like MaharajaDAO's DEX) or by earning them by becoming a part of the community and contributing to it's growth.

The MaharajaDAO will eventually have an ICO to fund it's activities.

Why value MAHA tokens?

The MAHA token not only represents voting power within the MaharajaDAO but it also receives tokens from invested projects which grows in value if the project becomes succesful.

Which means the more MAHA tokens you have, the more voting power and rewards you get.
