What is a DAO?

A DAO stands for a Decentralized Autonomus Organization. It is an organization that is fully decentralized with all the decisions taken democratically by the supporters of the DAO.

What will the MaharajaDAO do?

MaharajaDao will help support and promote DAOs and DApps that are specifically made in India.
