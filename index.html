What is a DAO?

A DAO stands for a Decentralized Autonomus Organization. It is a for-profit organization that is fully decentralized without any central authority in control. All the decisions are taken democratically, by the token holders of the DAO.

What will the MaharajaDAO do?

The MaharajaDao will support and promote DAOs and DApps that are specifically made in India.

By doing so, the MaharajaDao will help promote innovation and disruption in the country’s blockchain ecosystem.

Can the MaharajaDAO ever be taken down?

No. No entity or individual (not even the creators) can take full control of the MaharajaDAO.

By having power decentralized, the MaharajaDAO is highly resistant to corruption.
